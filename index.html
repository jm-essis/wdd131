Ivory Coast

Ivory Coast (officially Côte d'Ivoire) is a West African nation on the Gulf of Guinea, known as the world's leading producer of cocoa and cashew nuts. With a population of over 34 million, it is a culturally diverse republic with French as the official language. While its economic capital is the vibrant port city of Abidjan, the political capital is Yamoussoukro.
